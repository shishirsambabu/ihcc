03 — Why it's priced this way

Five reasons the number is fair.

1

It's custom — every line written by hand.

No WordPress theme, no drag-and-drop builder. All ~7,700 lines of styling were written specifically for IMPACT HCC. That means it's faster, more secure, impossible to mistake for a template ten thousand other firms use — and entirely yours.

2

It's bilingual — and Arabic runs right-to-left.

This isn't a translate button. Every layout mirrors, every line of copy is carried in a dictionary, and Google is told which language serves which region. It roughly doubles the testing. Agencies usually add 40–60% for a second language; here it's folded in.

3

It's a large site, not a five-pager.

Twenty-four pages — including eleven service pages and six articles built individually, not copy-pasted. More surface means more design, more content shaping, and more to keep consistent in both languages.

4

The invisible engineering is the expensive part.

Structured data, accessibility, performance and security are what make a site rank on Google, load instantly, work on every phone and stay safe. You never see them directly — but they decide whether the site actually performs.

5

You own it outright — no lock-in.

No monthly platform fee, no theme licence, no vendor holding your site hostage. You keep the code, and hosting for a site like this is close to free.
