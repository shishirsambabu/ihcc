IMPACT Human Capital Consultancy ("IMPACT HCC", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information we collect

This website does not use account registration, contact forms, or marketing cookies. We receive personal information only when you choose to contact us directly — for example by emailing office@impact-hcc.com or calling our offices. That information typically includes your name, organization, contact details, and the content of your message.

How we use it

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry, deliver services you request, and maintain our professional relationship. We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Third-party services

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts and embeds a Google Map on the Contact page. When your browser requests those resources, Google may process your IP address and browser information under its own privacy policy. Your language preference (English/Arabic) is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to us.

Data retention and your rights

We keep correspondence for as long as needed to serve the purpose it was provided for, or as required by UAE law. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by writing to office@impact-hcc.com.
